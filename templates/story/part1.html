You approach the house, led by the couple. They have told you on the way this house used to be owned by the woman's great-grand-parents. She presumes the riddle was left by the husband before he left on a trip abroad... from which he never returned.

You come in by the front door, which is quite low. Thankfully, the house has electric lighting, so a flick of the hand is that is needed to illuminate the main room, even if it is a bit dimly.

From the door, you can see the house is separated in three distinct areas. The first one would be the sitting area, close to the stove, where is a library along with a few couches and a coffee table. The second one would be the kitchen area, where a small counter space is set under a few small windows. A small round table with two chairs sits between the areas. The last area you can define is the sleeping area, partially hidden behind a wall and a door left ajar. You assume that the privvy is adjacent to the house, considering it small size.

I left you a surprise, my love, to find when you miss me. My obligations won't keep me away from you too long, I dare hope. You will find a treasure where:

  • White clad or not, you are a queen.
  • You inspire my every action.
  • I move brazenly towards you.
