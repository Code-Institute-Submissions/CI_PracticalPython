Introduction

"Over here, dear {{ username }}!"

You hear your next door neighbour from the street.

You are the best investigator in town. You have solved more cases than you can even count - maths was never your forte anyways. Your trusty {{ color }} umbrella in hand, you have braved spittle and deluge whenever called. Really, you would be right in thinking that you have been a {{ mystical }} for everyone troubled by an unexpected puzzle in this particular part of the world.

One day, you're approached by a couple who you have known all your life. They ask you for help regarding the most unusual of situations.

They have inherited a little cottage in the countryside from relatives. It looks a little spooky and dusty, but nonetheless quite charming. The previous owners have left furniture and decorations intact. It looks like a scene straight out of the early 20th century.

When the couple entered the cottage to tidy it up, their big dog Montague bumped into the mantelpiece and made a vase fall down. Inside it was a note:

I left you a surprise, my love, to find when you miss me. My patriotic obligations won't keep me away from you too long, I dare hope. Here is a riddle:
  • Time stops for our battles of wit.
  • White clad or not, you are a queen.
  • You inspire my every action.
  • I move brazenly towards you.
  • But you know how to stop my foolish plans.
  • I kneel in front of your beauty.
  • And carry the image of you with me.

You decide to help them and tag along to look at the house.
