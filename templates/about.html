About

In a nutshell: this is a short story where you get to look around an old house, take your time to enjoy it! I suggest you put on to classical or lo-fi music to enhance your reading experience.

You can access the story through the index page and the game information page. Your progress is displayed on the leaderboard page.

This is an online gamebook (or hypertext fictional story) inspired by the concept of 'choose your own adventure' classics. You call a gamebook a story that is written from a second-person point of view, with the reader assuming the role of the protagonist and making choices that determine the main character's actions and the plot's outcome. Hypertext fiction is the equivalent in electronic literature.

There are many great printed and online stories you can find on all subjects.
